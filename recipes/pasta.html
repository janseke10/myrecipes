Pasta Recipe

  • 1 pound pasta
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • garlic and onions

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain the pasta and return it to the pot. Cut the garlic and onion, and fry it until it's gold. Add the pasta sauce and grated parmesan cheese. Heat the pasta and sauce over low heat until the sauce is heated through and the cheese is melted. Serve hot. Enjoy!
