Brownies Recipe

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Preheat the oven to 200Â°C. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. In a large bowl, whisk together the vegetable oil, sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract until smooth. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips. Pour the batter into a greased 8-inch square baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
